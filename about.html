About Us

Baudm0n is an applied systems lab.

It exists to design, build, and document cyber-physical systems — machines where software, electronics, and mechanics converge into a single operating entity.

Baudm0n is not a brand and not a build channel.

It is a long-form engineering record.

The work documented here focuses on:

  • embedded control systems
  • power and sensing architectures
  • machine introspection and telemetry
  • human-machine interfaces
  • and the organization of subsystems into coherent, resilient entities

Philosophy

Most things that matter are not objects.

They are relationships.

A system is not defined by its parts, but by how its parts:

  • communicate
  • regulate
  • support
  • correct
  • and persist together

Baudm0n approaches machines the same way biology approaches organisms:

  • as layered, interacting subsystems that must remain coherent under stress, change, and failure.

The goal is not modification.

The goal is commissioning.

Why "Baudm0n"

A baud is a unit of signaling.

A daemon is a background system that sustains operation.

Baudm0n represents the intersection of:

  • communication
  • control
  • and persistence
